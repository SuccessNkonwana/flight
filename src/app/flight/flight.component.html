About SAFlights

1. Who are we

SAFlights.co.za is your go-to site for flight bookings from South Africa.
We’re a team of passionate, savvy travellers on a mission to make it easy for you to find the cheapest domestic and international flights.
As one of the world’s largest flight comparison websites, SAFlights is the starting point for your travel planning.

2. How do we do it?

Our innovative flight booking, curated deals and inspirational content make it simple to find cheap flights across South Africa.
We partner with hundreds of airlines and travel providers – all over South Africa - to bring you the cheapest flights and personalised travel options.
We then link you directly to the airlines or travel agents to book your flights and open up new travel possibilities to see the whole SA on a budget.

3. Why come to us?

Millions of travellers rely on us for trusted advice and the best selection of cheap flights and travel deals.
Our service is completely free - we will not charge you a rand.
We have been serving up useful tips, destination information and travel inspiration since 1996.
We know South Africa, we know the world. We combine experience with local knowledge and our global network of partners to create amazing travel moments that matter.
